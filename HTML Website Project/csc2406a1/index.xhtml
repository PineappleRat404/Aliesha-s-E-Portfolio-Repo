Promoting E-Transport in all forms

We are working together with industry leaders to make E-transport an accessible and affordable option for all Australians across all facets of life. Our purpose is to review companies that provide E-transport options to determine whether we can assist them in research, marketing, promotion and development. We're proud to encourage businesses to create sustainable solutions for consumers.

Fostering positive partnerships

Our main task is to review companies that manufacture and sell E-transportation products to ensure that they are delivering on promises made and are providing products that are safe, low / zero emission, and are affordable for consumers. We also carry out extensive reviews on individual products to confirm that these are compliant with safety standards and contain the features they claim to have. In this way, we can encourage greater transparency and competition, ensuring a wider range of E-vehicles in the marketplace for better accessibility.

Engaging all industries

The modern age we live in provides a high standard of living for most Australians. Unfortunately, these high standards come with higher emissions of greenhouse gases into our atmosphere. In order to reduce these, we need to direct our focus to all forms of transportation. This is why our stringent review processes aim to cover every form of E-transportation possible: so everyone can enjoy emission-free transport at an affordable price, no matter the activity.
