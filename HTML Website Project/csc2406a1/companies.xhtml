Our partners in Innovation

We have reviewed the E-transport products offered by these companies to ensure they are sustainable and affordable options. Our aim is to review as many different transport options as possible in order to cater to all consumer needs. We encourage you to read more about these companies and review their products for yourself.

John Deere

John Deere is an industry leader in agricultural machinery. As a company, they have made great strides in the development of self-driving machines and smart technologies that have largely automated the most manual aspects of agricultural labour. To create a more sustainable future, our department works closely with industry leaders like John Deere to promote the development of E-transport that is zero-emission and better for the planet. As we now know, the agricultural industry is a major contributor to global emissions and we are proud to work with a business striving to reduce these.

John Deere has produced a range of E-vehicles for domestic and commercial use:

  • Types: Tractors, Utility vehicles, Mowers
  • Variants: Commercial, Domestic

Visit our John Deere page for information about their products.


Eco Boats Australia

The Australian lifestyle demands a range of domestic vehicles that have zero emissions but can also provide the same level of performance and convenience that we are accustomed to. Eco Boats is an Australian company that manufactures E-transport designed for use in our oceans and local waterways. They have developed a range of boats and electic motors that provide a safer and emission-free mode of transport. This means that new watercraft create less pollution and existing boats can be retro-fitted to be more environmentally conscious.

Eco Boats has produced a range of E-vehicles for domestic and commercial use:

  • Types: Yachts, Catamarans, Tour boats
  • Variants: Tourism, Luxury, Domestic

Visit our Eco Boats Australia page for more information about their products.


Hyundai

Hyundai is among one of the leading car brands that has a wide range of E-transport options available on the market. It is our mission to work with electric car manufacturers to widen the range of vehicles available to make them more affordable for everyday Australians. Hyundai has already developed a range of hybrid and electric vehicles and they strive to expand their range of options even further in the near future.

Hyundai has produced a range of E-vehicles for domestic and commercial use:

  • Types: SUV's, Sedans
  • Variants: Luxury, Economy, Domestic

Visit our Hyundai page for more information about their products.


Savic Motorcycles

Savic Motorcycles is a newcomer to the E-transport market, but they promise disruptive innovation in the local market for E-motorcycles. Their products are designed and built in Australia and are highly customisable. While their range currently isn't large, the customisations they offer ensure that you not only have an environmentally friendly transport option but also a unique one. Our department encourages collaboration with local manufacturers to create jobs and produce clean transport for every Australian.

Savic Motorcycles has produced a range of E-vehicles for domestic use:

  • Types: Motorcycles
  • Variants: Luxury, Domestic

Visit our Savic Motorcycles page for more information about their products.


Volvo Trucks

Volvo is a brand known across the globe for producing safe and reliable vehicles. They have recently expanded into the E-transport market with their range of electric trucks designed for commercial use. They are a brand that promises reliability in freight transport, while being environmentally conscious. We have reviewed Volvo's E-transport products in the hope that uptake on green freight will increase nationally, promoting other manufacturers to create more options in the marketplace.

Volvo Trucks has produced a range of E-Vehicles for commercial use:

  • Types: Trucks, Heavy Vechicles
  • Variants: Commercial

Visit our Volvo Trucks page for more information about their products.
