Eco Boats Australia

Eco Boats Australia is a leading provider in electric boats and boating equipment. Established in Australia in 2008, Eco Boats offers an emission-free alternative to petrol-driven boating. In addition to E-boats, they also offer equipment (such as batteries and solar panels) that can be used to retrofit existing watercraft (Eco Boats Australia, 2025). This allows consumers to be environmentally conscious at a fraction of the cost if they already own a boat. We have been reviewing Eco Boats products since 2020 in an effort to promote a wider range of E-transport options.


What products have we reviewed?

As part of our review process, we consider the range of E-transport options a company has available and also review these separately. The aim of this is to ensure each product we promote is safe for use and is either low-emission or emission free. You can view Eco Boats Australia's reviewed products below:

  • Cat 16 Electric Catamaran

    • The Cat 16 provides a luxury product to the everyday boating enthusiast that is environmentally friendly. Made in the USA, this model offers a cruise time of 6 hours or 4 hours at top speed (5 knots). With whisper-quiet operation it offers the same performance as comparable petrol-driven products. It also features a digital fuel gauge that you can use to keep track of the boat's battery life (Eco Boats Australia, 2025).

  • SoelCat 12

    • The SoelCat 12 is part of Eco Boats' range of solar-powered yachts and can be used for tour operations. Mounted with solar panels, it provides smooth running for night time cruises or in times of cloudy weather (Eco Boats Australia, 2025). It provides and eco-friendly operation not only for tourism operators but will also appeal to customers looking for a sustainable holiday experience.
